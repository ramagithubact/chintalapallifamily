Our Legacy

The Chintalapalli Family Website is a humble and heartfelt tribute to the distinguished lineage of our forefathers who laid the foundation for our present. It is created as a lasting digital monument to honor and remember their lives, their values, and their enduring legacy.

At the heart of our family heritage stand our revered ancestors: Chintalapalli Peddappayya, Chintalapalli Peddaveerenkatappa, and Chintalapalli Chinnaveerenkatappa. These three pioneering figures, bonded through blood and legacy, are the earliest known patriarchs of the Chintalapalli lineage, all of whom resided in the serene village of Venkatampalli.

Peddappayya’s lineage continued through his son Ramappa, who in turn was the proud father of Masthanappa, Bhoomireddy, and Subbaiah. Parallelly, Peddaveerenkatappa's legacy extended through his son Masthanappa, and Chinnaveerenkatappa through his son Nagireddy. Each of these families contributed significantly to the growth, integrity, and cultural wealth of the Chintalapalli family.

All of these noble ancestors, now no longer with us, are regarded with immense respect as the Godfathers of the Chintalapalli Family Dynasty. Their memory lives on not only in the stories passed down through generations but also in the very values and traditions we cherish today.

While most descendants continue to reside in the ancestral village of Venkatampalli, the family line of Subbaiah made a new beginning in Indlavenkatampalli after his marriage, and his children and grandchildren remain settled there to this day.

This website is more than a digital archive — it is a celebration of legacy, a bridge between generations, and a symbol of unity. Through this platform, we aim to:

  • Preserve and document the family tree for future generations
  • Foster stronger connections among extended family members
  • Honor the memory of those who came before us
  • Celebrate the cultural and historical roots of our community

We sincerely thank every member of the Chintalapalli family for their presence, pride, and participation. May this website serve as a reminder of our shared past and a beacon for our collective future.
